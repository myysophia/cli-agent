# Daily Market Overview Workflow

## 10-Minute Daily Routine

### Time Allocation
- **0-2 min**: Index check
- **2-4 min**: News scan
- **4-7 min**: Watchlist review
- **7-9 min**: Chart analysis
- **9-10 min**: Risk assessment & action plan

### Best Practices

1. **Consistency**: Do it at the same time every day
2. **Focus**: Don't try to analyze everything
3. **Documentation**: Keep a daily log
4. **Review**: Weekly summary of patterns
5. **Adapt**: Adjust routine based on effectiveness

### Common Patterns

**Bullish Market**:
- Indices trending up
- Volume increasing
- News mostly positive
- Strategy: Look for opportunities

**Bearish Market**:
- Indices trending down
- Volume decreasing
- News mostly negative
- Strategy: Preserve capital

**Sideways Market**:
- Indices flat
- Volume normal
- Mixed news
- Strategy: Trade ranges, be selective

### Red Flags to Watch

- Sudden volume spikes
- Major index breaks key levels
- Unexpected policy changes
- Multiple stocks hitting limits
- Extreme sentiment readings

### Weekly Review Questions

2. What patterns emerged?

4. What should I change?
5. What worked well?
